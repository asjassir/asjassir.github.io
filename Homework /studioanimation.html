About Us

    SLAKE Magazine is an online platform for international Arts & Culture. SLAKE publishes a weekly feature on choice painters, sculptors, print-makers, photographers, and sound artists. Mission: To provide a platform to showcase and promote the work of emerging artists and arts organisations. Each article is handpicked and curated from submissions from around the world. Publishing Schedule: New articles are published every Sunday (00:01 GMT). Follow SLAKE to receive a weekly email when new content is published. SLAKE Magazine launched in September 2014 and is edited by Nicola Joanne Carter.
